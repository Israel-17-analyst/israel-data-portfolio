Shipping Company Sales Performance Dashboard

Tool: Excel and Power BI Desktop
Dataset: Fictional shipping company revenue data (Excel and cleaned using excel power query) , 2003–2005
Coverage: 2003 (Q1–Q4) | 2004 (Q1–Q4) | 2005 (Q1–Q2 only)
Breakdown by: Year, Quarter, Region, Country

Features

Year & quarter slicers for flexible filtering
Revenue breakdown by region and country
Year-over-year and quarter-over-quarter comparison
Monthly trend isolation within each year


Key Findings

$3.2M gap between peak (2004) and lowest (2005) revenue periods
November revenue spike in both 2003 and 2004 — consistent with Q4 holiday shipping/logistics demand
EMEA generated the highest revenue as a region with $4.6M
U.S.A generated the highest revenue as a country with $3.1M generated from there
The Classic cars were the best selling products generating $3.6M in total
2005 decline is partly a data coverage artifact — only 2 of 4 quarters recorded, not a confirmed full-year business collapse
